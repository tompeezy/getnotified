📜 Family Internet Rules Agreement

🏠 The ___________ Family

Internet & Device Safety Agreement • ___________
ℹ️ We agree to follow these rules to keep our family safe, happy, and healthy online.
  • 1
    Screen Time Limits: We agree to follow our family's daily screen time limits and put devices away during meals, family time, and 1 hour before bedtime.
  • 2
    Private Information: I will NEVER share my real name, address, school, phone number, or photos with people I don't know online.
  • 3
    App & Website Approval: I will ask a parent before downloading any new app, game, or visiting new websites. No exceptions.
  • 4
    No Cyberbullying: I will be kind online. I will not say anything online that I wouldn't say to someone's face. I will tell a parent if I see bullying.
  • 5
    Tell a Trusted Adult: If anything online makes me feel scared, uncomfortable, or confused, I will immediately tell Mom, Dad, or another trusted adult — no matter what.
  • 6
    Devices in Common Areas: I will use devices in shared family spaces (living room, kitchen). No devices in bedrooms after 9:00 PM.
  • 7
    Passwords are Private: I will not share passwords with friends. Only parents know my device passwords.
  • 8
    Online Friends Stay Online: I will never agree to meet someone in person that I only know from online, without a parent present.
  • 9
    No In-App Purchases: I will ask a parent before making any in-app purchase, subscription, or buying anything online.
  • 10
    Consequences: If I break these rules, I understand my device privileges may be reduced or removed. This is to keep me safe, not to punish me.
