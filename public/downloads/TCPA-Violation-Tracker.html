📋 TCPA Quick Reference — Know Your Rights

  • $500/text for unsolicited marketing texts. $1,500/text if willful.
  • After replying STOP: company has 10 business days to honor it. Any text after = violation.
  • You can sue in small claims court — no lawyer needed for cases under $10k.
  • Take screenshots with timestamps. Save everything. That's your evidence.
  • Most companies settle immediately when you send a demand letter.

🏛️ Small Claims Filing Guide (NC)

    

      

        
Step 1 — Send Demand Letter

        
Give them 14 days to settle. Most do. Use the generator above.

      

      

        
Step 2 — File in Small Claims

        
NC Small Claims: up to $10,000. File at your county courthouse. $96 filing fee.

      

      

        
Step 3 — Serve the Defendant

        
Look up company's registered agent on NC Secretary of State website. Serve them.

      

      

        
Step 4 — Show Up & Win

        
Bring screenshots, timestamps, STOP confirmation. Judges love TCPA cases. You usually win.

      

    

    

      💡 Pro tip: Companies often settle before court when they receive a demand letter. $500 is cheaper than their legal fees.
